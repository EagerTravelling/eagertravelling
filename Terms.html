  • Unless such stated in your medical questionnaire, you must confirm that you are in excellent health and physically capable of participating in all components of the trip at the time of booking. If you are unable to provide this confirmation for any reason, or if you have a medical condition or disability that may affect your trip, please contact us before submitting your application so that we can assist you in determining the trip's suitability for you.
