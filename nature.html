Nature

Norway

Reinebringen


Reinebringen is located in the Lofoten Islands, far up North in Norway. This idyllic hike is one of the most popular treks in the area. It offers views over Reine, Sakrisøy, and Hamnøy fishing villages.The hike is only 3 km in distance
Duration time: 3 hours
From MYR 643.00 per adult



China

Luoping Canola Flower Ocean,Beijing

Luoping County is home to extensive rapeseed production and the flowers are harvested to produce a range of products. The region's rolling fields are interspersed among short, karst-like mountains. During peak bloom, the entire region appears to be one big yellow carpet.
Open from Jan 1 to Dec 31
sightseeing time:1-2 days
From MYR 200.00 per person
