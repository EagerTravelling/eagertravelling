Island and Ocean

Hawaii

Snorkel in Hanauma Bay, Oahu


O'ahu Bay is the most popular tourist destination on the island of O'ahu in Hawaii. It is also a Nature Preserve, since it features a very rich and versatile marine life. Pristine marine ecosystem is indeed impressive and therefore protected by the state. The bay is incredibly beautiful, besides a rich coral reef provides a great opportunity to discover the entire underwater world.
Duration time: 4.00pm to 6.30pm
From MYR 500.00 per adult

Hawaii

Windsurfing in Kailua Beach

Kailua Beach Park is one of O'ahu's top windsurfing spots for both novice and experienced windsurfers. Set out over the crystal-clear waters of Kailua Bay from Kailua Beach Park or Lanikai Beach. The uninhabited Mokulua Islands are seabird refuge islands where endemic Hawaiian petrels can be seen.The wind on the harbour is usually light and playful, and Kailua Beach is one of O'ahu's top beaches for windsurfing.
Duration Times: 4 hours
From MYR 200.00 per person

Costa Rica

Kayak in Damas Island

The Damas Island Mangroves are located just south of Manuel Antonio and Quepos. There is no shortage of wildlife and amazing plant life with its unique ability to survive in salt water. This tour can be done either by boat or kayak and is fun & educational.
Activity Duration: 2.5 hours to 3 hours
From MYR 275.28 per person
